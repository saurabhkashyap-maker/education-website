Our Story

Shiksha Support, a unit of Jha Classes, was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision - to make quality education accessible to every student in India. What started as a small initiative by a group of passionate educators has now grown into a comprehensive learning platform serving thousands of students across the country. The name "Shiksha Support" combines two Hindi words - "Shiksha" (education) and "Support" - reflecting our commitment to supporting students in their educational journey. We truly believe in building bridges between students and quality education, between learners and expert faculty, between dreams and achievements.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has access to personalized, quality education regardless of their location or background. We envision a future where learning is not confined by geographical boundaries but is a seamless, engaging experience available to all.
